About BLT University

Empowering the next generation of security professionals through community-driven, open-source education.

Our Mission

BLT University is a revolutionary open-source educational platform dedicated to making cybersecurity education accessible to everyone. We believe that knowledge should be free, collaborative, and community-driven.

By leveraging GitHub's powerful collaboration features, we've created a unique learning environment where students learn through doing and instructors teach through transparent pull requests.

How It Works

GitHub-Powered

All courses are hosted on GitHub Pages, making them version-controlled and transparent.

Collaborative Teaching

Instructors update curriculum via Pull Requests, enabling community peer review.

Automated Workflows

GitHub Actions handle enrollment and certificate generation automatically.

Community-Driven

Practitioners and students collectively build and maintain the best curriculum possible.

Our Core Values

  • Open Source Content is open and free to modify under AGPL-3.0.
  • Practical Learning Focused on real-world, hands-on security skills.
