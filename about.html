About BLT University

Open-Source Cybersecurity Education for Everyone

Our Mission

BLT University is a revolutionary open-source educational platform dedicated to making cybersecurity education accessible to everyone. We believe that security knowledge should be free, collaborative, and community-driven.

By leveraging GitHub's powerful collaboration features, we've created a unique learning environment where students learn through doing, instructors teach through pull requests, and the entire community contributes to the growth of knowledge.
